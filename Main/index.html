About Me

With a degree in English from Guangdong University of Foreign Studies, where I also studied Spanish as a second language, I have a strong linguistic foundation. My education includes an exchange program at the National University of Singapore, further enhancing my global perspective.

My expertise extends beyond the classroom. I have extensive experience working as a translator and interpreter in various industries, including foreign trade, factory visits, business negotiations, and international exhibitions. Having lived and worked with English and Spanish-speaking professionals, I have honed my communication skills for diverse cultural contexts. Additionally, my role as a retail anchor on TikTok in the US market showcases my fluency in English.
